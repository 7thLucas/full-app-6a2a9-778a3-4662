Build an AI-powered Audit & Compliance Management application that helps organizations conduct audits, inspections, and compliance reviews more efficiently.

Administrators can upload SOPs, policies, standards, and compliance documents. The system should automatically extract requirements and create audit checklists that reviewers can use during inspections.

Auditors can perform assessments by answering questions, uploading evidence such as documents or photos, and recording findings. The system should help reviewers determine whether each requirement passes or fails and identify areas that need improvement.

When issues are found, the platform should automatically create corrective action items, assign owners, track progress, and monitor resolution status.

Key capabilities:

* Upload SOPs and compliance documents
* Generate audit checklists
* Configure audit rules
* Collect evidence through forms and file uploads
* AI-assisted compliance reviews
* Pass/fail assessments
* Issue and finding management
* Corrective action tracking
* Audit history and reporting
* Compliance dashboards

The goal is to replace spreadsheets and manual audits with a centralized platform that helps teams maintain compliance, identify risks, and resolve issues faster.
