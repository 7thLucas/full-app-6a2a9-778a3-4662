# Audra — Internal Audit & Compliance Platform

## What it is
A centralized platform that replaces spreadsheets and manual audits. Internal teams conduct audits, inspections, and compliance reviews; the system extracts requirements from uploaded SOPs/standards into checklists, supports live inspections with evidence capture, AI-assisted pass/fail review, and turns every issue into a tracked corrective action.

## Users
- **Administrators** — upload SOPs, policies, standards, and compliance documents; configure audit rules; the system extracts requirements and generates audit checklists.
- **Auditors / Reviewers** — run live inspections from a checklist; answer questions, upload evidence (documents and photos), record findings, mark each requirement pass/fail.
- **Action owners** — assigned corrective actions; track progress and resolution status.
Internal-facing only (not a client/vendor portal).

## Day-one centerpiece (build this first and unmistakably well)
The **live auditor experience**: a reviewer opens an audit checklist and works through each requirement — answering questions, uploading evidence (docs/photos), recording findings, and marking pass/fail, with AI-assisted compliance review flagging gaps and areas needing improvement. The inspection must feel smooth and fast end to end.

## Supporting capabilities (wire around the centerpiece)
- Upload SOPs and compliance documents; AI extracts requirements and generates audit checklists.
- Configure audit rules.
- Collect evidence through forms and file uploads.
- AI-assisted compliance reviews; pass/fail assessments.
- Issue and finding management.
- Corrective action tracking: auto-create action items from failed/issue requirements, assign owners, track progress and resolution status.
- Audit history and reporting; compliance dashboards.

## Strategic principle
The real value is closing the loop — findings cannot quietly die. Repeat findings are what turn into fines and lost certifications, so corrective actions must be tracked to resolution. The AI must save auditor hours on checklist creation and gap-spotting.

## Tone / brand
Trustworthy, calm, enterprise-grade. Auditors must trust it on sight — nothing playful.
