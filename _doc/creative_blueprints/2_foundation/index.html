Why this exists

The Problem

Audits live in spreadsheets and scattered documents.

  • — Building a checklist from a dense SOP is slow and manual.
  • — Evidence is scattered across emails, photos, and folders.
  • — Pass/fail judgment is inconsistent and undefensible.
  • — Corrective actions slip — driving repeat findings, fines, and lost certifications.

The Solution

One centralized platform from document to resolution.

  • + AI extracts requirements from SOPs and generates the checklist.
  • + Auditors capture evidence and AI-assisted pass/fail in the field.
  • + Every failure becomes an owned, tracked corrective action.
  • + History, dashboards, and reporting give a defensible audit trail.
