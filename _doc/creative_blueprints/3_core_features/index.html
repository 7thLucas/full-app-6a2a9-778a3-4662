Core Features

Built around three roles

Compliance Admin Auditor / Reviewer Action Owner
1

Upload source documents

Bring in SOPs, policies, standards, and compliance docs as the source of truth.

2

AI-generated checklists

AI extracts the underlying requirements and turns them into structured audit checklists.

3

Configure audit rules

Tune how requirements are scored so audits match your standards.

4

Evidence collection

Auditors answer questions and upload documents and photos as evidence in the field.

5

AI-assisted pass/fail

AI-assisted reviews help determine pass/fail and flag areas needing improvement.

6

Corrective action tracking

Findings spawn action items with owners, progress tracking, and resolution status.

Plus: audit history & reporting and compliance dashboards across the organization.
