# Audra — Design Guidelines
- **Palette**: Confident deep blues as primary, calm neutral grays, white surfaces. A single restrained accent for status (green pass / amber needs-improvement / red fail). Credibility over flash.
- **Typography**: Clean, legible sans-serif. Clear hierarchy for checklist items, requirements, and findings. Comfortable line length for reading SOP-derived requirements.
- **Layout**: Generous whitespace; dense-but-orderly tables for audit history and corrective actions; a focused, distraction-free inspection view for auditors working a checklist on the go (mobile-friendly evidence capture).
- **Components**: Pass/fail toggles, evidence upload (file + photo), finding notes, progress/status badges for corrective actions, compliance dashboard cards. Elevation subtle; rounded corners modest.
- **Feel**: Apple HIG-level clarity — confident, quiet, professional. Nothing toy-like.
